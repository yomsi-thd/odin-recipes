Ingredients

Main Ingredients 🍽️🎀

  • 🌾 Flour – 1 cup, for a smooth and light batter
  • 🥚 Eggs – 2, for structure and softness
  • 🥛 Milk – 1 cup, to make it silky smooth
  • 🧈 Butter – 2 tbsp, melted, for richness
  • 🍯 Sugar – 1 tbsp, for a hint of sweetness
  • 🧂 Salt – A pinch, to balance flavors

Toppings & Fillings 🍓✨

  • 🍓 Fresh strawberries – Sweet and juicy!
  • 🍌 Banana slices – Adds lovely texture
  • 🍫 Chocolate sauce – Because everything is better with chocolate!
  • 🍯 Honey or syrup – Perfect for a dreamy finish
  • 🍦 Whipped cream – Light and fluffy
  • 🌟 Powdered sugar – A touch of elegance

Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Batter 🍚🎀

  • In a bowl, whisk together 🥚 eggs, 🥛 milk, and 🧈 melted butter.
  • Sift in 🌾 flour, 🍯 sugar, and a 🧂 pinch of salt.
  • Mix gently until smooth—don’t overmix to keep it light and airy!

Step 2: Cooking the Crepes 🔥🍽️

  • Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat and lightly grease it with 🧈 butter.
  • Pour a thin layer of batter, swirling the pan to spread evenly. ✨
  • Cook for 1-2 minutes, until the edges slightly lift.
  • Carefully flip and cook for another minute until golden brown.

Step 3: Decorating & Serving 🍓💖✨

  • Fill with 🍓 fresh strawberries, 🍌 banana slices, and 🍦 whipped cream!
  • Drizzle 🍫 chocolate sauce or 🍯 honey for sweetness.
  • Sprinkle 🌟 powdered sugar and fold elegantly.
  • Add a cute heart 🎀 using syrup for the full maid café charm! 💖
