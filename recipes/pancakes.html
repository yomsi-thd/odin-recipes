Ingredients

Main Ingredients🍽️

  • 🥚 Eggs – 2, for a fluffy texture
  • 🥛 Milk – ¾ cup, to make it smooth
  • 🌾 Flour – 1 cup, for the perfect fluffiness
  • 🍯 Sugar – 2 tbsp, for sweetness
  • 🧂 Salt – A pinch, to enhance flavor
  • 🛢️ Baking powder – 1 tsp, for rising magi
  • 🧈 Butter – 2 tbsp, melted, for richness

Toppings & Decorations 🍓✨

  • 🍓 Strawberries – Fresh and sweet!
  • 🍌 Banana – Sliced for extra cuteness
  • 🍦 Whipped cream – Fluffy and dreamy
  • 🍫 Chocolate syrup – Because why not? 🍫
  • 🌟 Powdered sugar – For an elegant touch

Steps

Step 1: Prepare the Batter 🍚✨

  1. In a bowl, whisk together 🥚 eggs, 🥛 milk, and 🧈 melted butter.
  2. Sift in 🌾 flour, 🛢️ baking powder, 🍯 sugar, and a 🧂 pinch of salt.
  3. Mix gently until the batter is smooth—not overmixed to keep it fluffy! 🥞

Step 2: Cooking the Pancakes 🔥🍽️

  1. Heat a non-stick pan over medium-low heat and lightly grease it with 🧈 butter.
  2. Pour a scoop of batter and let it spread into a perfect round. 🌀✨
  3. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip carefully with a spatula! 🔄🥞
  4. Cook for another minute until golden brown, then transfer to a plate. 🍽️🎀

Step 3: Decorating & Serving 🍓💖✨

  1. Stack the pancakes neatly, adding whipped cream 🍦 between layers.
  2. Arrange 🍓 fresh strawberries and 🍌 banana slices on top.
  3. Drizzle 🍫 chocolate syrup or 🍯 honey for an extra dreamy touch! 😍✨
  4. Sprinkle 🌟 powdered sugar and draw a cute face 🎀 using syrup!
