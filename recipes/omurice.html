Ingredients

For the Fried Rice 🍚🔥

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • ½ small onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup diced chicken (or ham, shrimp, or tofu)
  • ¼ cup mixed vegetables (peas, corn, carrots)
  • 2 tbsp ketchup (plus extra for decoration)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Omelet 🍳💛

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional, for a fluffier texture)
  • ½ tbsp butter or oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

Prepare the Fried Rice 🍚🔥

  1. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Sauté chopped onions until soft.
  3. Add diced chicken (or your preferred protein) and cook until it's no longer pink.
  4. Toss in mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  5. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  6. Pour in ketchup and soy sauce, then stir everything together until evenly coated.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and set aside.

Make the Omelet 🍳💛

  1. Whisk eggs and milk (optional) in a bowl until combined.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium-low heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan, tilting it slightly to spread evenly.
  4. Let the omelet cook until the edges set, but keep the center slightly runny.

Assemble the Omurice 🍽️🎀

  1. Place the cooked fried rice in the center of the omelet.
  2. Carefully fold the omelet over the rice, shaping it into an oval.
  3. Gently transfer to a plate, using a spatula to help shape it neatly.

Decorate & Serve 🍽️💖

  1. Drizzle ketchup on top in cute designs or messages.
  2. Garnish with parsley, cheese, or demi-glace sauce if desired.
  3. Serve warm and enjoy your homemade omurice! ✨
